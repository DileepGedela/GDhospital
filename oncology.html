Dr. Pushpinder Gulia


Dr. Pushpinder Gulia is a Surgical Oncologist in Gurgaon Sector 57, Gurgaon and has an experience of 21 years in this field. Dr. Pushpinder Gulia practices at Dental Krafts in Gurgaon Sector 57, Gurgaon,Eye Care Clinic in Gurgaon Sector 31, Gurgaon and Fortis Memorial Research Institute in Gurgaon Sector 44, Gurgaon. He completed MBBS from Indira Gandhi Medical College, Shimla in 2003,MS - General Surgery from Indira Gandhi Medical College, Shimla in 2007 and DNB - Surgical Oncology from Army College of Medical Sciences, New Delhi in 2010. He is a member of Indian Association of Surgical Oncology (IASO) and Association of Surgeons of India (ASI). Some of the services provided by the doctor are: Sentinel Node Biopsy,Minor Surgery,Minimally Invasive Surgery,Breast Cancer Treatment and Mastectomy etc.
