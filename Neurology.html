She is board certified by the American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology and is a member of the American Academy of Neurology. Dr. Waldron completed her medical internship and neurology residency at New York University Medical Center, where she underwent training at three major institutions: Bellevue Hospital, Tisch Hospital (now NYU Langone Med ical Center) and the Manhattan Veterans Administration Hospital. She was appointed one of the neurology department’s chief residents from 2009-2010. She attended New York University School of Medicine where she earned her d octorate in medicine and received the American Academy of Neurology “Excellence in Neurology” award at graduation. Dr. Waldron graduated summa cum laude with a Bachelor of Science degree in biology from Boston College.
