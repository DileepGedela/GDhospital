Dr. Naveen Kumar Verma


Dr. Naveen Kumar Verma is a General Surgeon,Bariatric Surgeon and Laparoscopic Surgeon in Dwarka Sector 6, Delhi and has an experience of 9 years in these fields. Dr. Naveen Kumar Verma practices at Manipal Hospital in Dwarka Sector 6, Delhi. He completed MBBS from Maharshi Dayanand 'University, Rohtak in 2010 and MS - General Surgery from Pt. B D Sharma Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences, Rohtak in 2015. He is a member of Delhi Medical Council. Some of the services provided by the doctor are: Non-Surgical Fat Loss, Breast Augmentation Surgery,Breast Reduction Surgery,Minimally Invasive Surgery and Cosmetic Surgery etc.
